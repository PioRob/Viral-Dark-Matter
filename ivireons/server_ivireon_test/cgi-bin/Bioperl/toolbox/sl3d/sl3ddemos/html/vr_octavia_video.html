Vehicle Dynamics Visualization with Video Output

This example extends the vr_octavia example and shows how to use video output from the VR Video Output block.

This demo performs a few simple operations on the output video signal. The Video Viewer display then shows the resulting video stream where the image edges are detected.

This demo requires the Video and Image Processing Blockset™ product.
